# Concept Brief: "Bird Mirror" Rocket Identity

**Status:** Concept Phase
**Inspiration:** "Arbalest Red Robin" (See attached image: `Arbalest_Red_Robin.png`)
**Target Aesthetic:** High-Gloss / Chrome / Reflective

## The Vision
We want to move away from the "standard white rocket" and explore a **"Bird Mirror"** aesthetic.
*   **Finish:** Highly reflective chrome or polished metal (Mirror finish).
*   **Effect:** The rocket body should reflect the environment (sky, clouds, launch rail).
*   **Accents:** Use **Astra Red (#FF3333)** for the fins, nose cone, or vector stripes.

## Why this works for Pioneer?
*   It feels "futuristic" and "premium" (not just a PVC tube).
*   The "Bird" motif aligns with flight/freedom.
*   It stands out distinctly on the launch pad against the desert/sky.
