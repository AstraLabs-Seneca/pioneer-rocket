# ASTRA Labs: Marketing & Outreach

## Content Structure
- **Branding:** Logos, color palettes, fonts.
- **Campaigns:** Social media content calendars, event flyers.
- **Media:** Photos and videos for press releases.

## Use Cases
- Storing assets for the website and social media.
- Drafting press releases and blog posts.

## Access Governance
- Designer access policy: `docs/marketing/DESIGNER_ACCESS.md`

## AI Policy (Design Only)
- AI is allowed for visual concept exploration and mockups in:
  - `assets/Astra_Inspo`
  - `assets/DJ_Style`
  - `assets/concepts`
- Final campaign and official voice decisions remain human-owned.
- Operating rule:
  - Primary: `HUMAN_ONLY` designer assets first.
  - Backup: AI concepts only when blocked by time or capacity.
  - Final approval: human decision only.

## 0 AI Section (Human-Only)
- `assets/HUMAN_ONLY` is strictly no-AI content.
- Allowed in `HUMAN_ONLY`:
  - Human-created writing
  - Human-drawn design assets
  - Human-edited final copy
- Not allowed in `HUMAN_ONLY`:
  - AI-generated text
  - AI-generated images
  - AI-assisted rewrites
